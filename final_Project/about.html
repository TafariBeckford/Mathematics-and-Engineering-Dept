About Us..

Join us in bulding a better world

We are fun and quirky, elite but not elitist, inventive and artistic, obsessed with numbers, and welcoming to talented people regardless of where they come from. The University is committed to generating, disseminating, and preserving knowledge, and to working with others to bring this knowledge to bear on the world’s great challenges.

NCU is dedicated to providing its students with an education that combines rigorous academic study and the excitement of discovery with the support and intellectual stimulation of a diverse campus community. We seek to develop in each member of the community the ability and passion to work wisely, creatively, and effectively for the betterment of humankind.

Key Facts

Our Motto

Ubi Semper Discimus – (Where learning never ends). There is always something new to learn about in mathematics and engineering which spark new ideas.

Mission Statement

The NCU Mathematics and Engineering Department exist to provide quality, Christ-centered, industrially relevant, AAA/ABET/UCJ accredited mathematics and engineering training programmes at the graduate and undergraduate levels for Jamaican, CARICOM and foreign nationals, underpinned by globally innovative research, analysis, design, and guided by internationally relevant standards

Vision Statement

The NCU Mathematics and Engineering Department aspires to become the premier CARICOM based tertiary institution for holistic, Christ-centered, mathematics&engineering training. Consequently it will become the central regional research hub for translational innovation of market relevant engineering concepts into profitable engineering start-up enterprises that spur natural growth and development.
